Buhari commissions Seme Border Post

President Muhammadu Buhari has commissioned the newly constructed Seme-Krake joint border post between Nigeria and the Republic of Benin. The President commissioned the border post on Tuesday, July 23, 2021, during his one-day official visit to the Republic of Benin.
